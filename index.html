About

Hi, I'm Matthieu, a Junior DevOps Engineer focused on automation, CI/CD, infrastructure provisioning, and observability. I'm currently completing the “Concepteur développeur de solutions informatiques” certification at 42.

Through two six-month DevOps internships at bioMérieux, I contributed to the DevOps infrastructure of 3P Connect, a web application used for environmental monitoring and traceability in pharmaceutical and industrial contexts. My work included migrating Jenkins pipelines to GitLab CI/CD, containerizing automated tests, provisioning integration environments on vSphere, and deploying Prometheus/Grafana observability dashboards.
